# 🚑 ResQAI
## AI-Powered Volunteer & Resource Coordination Platform

ResQAI is an AI-driven volunteer coordination platform designed to help NGOs and community organizations respond quickly to urgent local needs such as disasters, relief operations, medical camps, and community support activities.

The platform centralizes community data and intelligently connects volunteers with tasks where they are needed most using AI-based matching and recommendations.

---

## 🌍 Problem

NGOs and social organizations collect valuable community information through surveys, field reports, and volunteer registrations. However, this information is often scattered across spreadsheets and manual reports, making it difficult to:

- Identify urgent community needs  
- Coordinate volunteers efficiently  
- Deploy resources quickly during emergencies  

---

## 💡 Solution

ResQAI provides a centralized platform that enables organizations to manage volunteers, tasks, and resources while using AI to recommend the most suitable volunteers for each task.

The system improves coordination speed, reduces manual effort, and enables data-driven decision making.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- Volunteer registration and management  
- CSV import for volunteer databases  
- Task creation from community reports  
- AI-powered volunteer matching  
- Gemini AI recommendation engine  
- Resource prioritization system  
- Operational dashboard with metrics  
- Volunteer location visualization using a map  

---

## 🤖 AI Matching Logic

Volunteers are ranked using a weighted scoring model:

Volunteer Score =  
40% Skill Match  
30% Location Proximity  
30% Availability  

The AI matching engine selects the best volunteers based on these factors.

Gemini AI then generates a natural language explanation describing why specific volunteers were recommended.
